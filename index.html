About Sol & Mantle

Sol & Mantle LLP is a boutique legal startup built for a connected, evolving world. Rooted in timeless legal values and powered by technology, we deliver agile, client-centered legal services to individuals, startups, and growing enterprises—wherever they are.

Founder: Adv. Lalchhanhima Chhangte

Sol & Mantle LLP was founded with a vision to modernize legal practice without compromising its ethical foundation. Adv. Lalchhanhima Chhangte leads the firm with a focus on responsibility, strategic thinking, and trust. Under his leadership, Sol & Mantle has grown into a lean, impact-driven legal practice, serving clients across jurisdictions with integrity at its core.
